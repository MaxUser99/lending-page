What is SWARM?

We want to offer the world out vision of this product. DeFi Aggregator with the development of its own protocol.

Own DeFi Protocol Aggregator specifically designed to help the investor access the best cryptocurrency Defi rates and prices, easily and efficiently, no matter where they are.

Our DeFi Protocol Aggregator is governed by a blockchain-based Decentralized Autonomous Organization. also known as DAO.

Features

Click, done. Sit back and watch your assets grow. Leave the heavy-lifting to the code.

Best Yield

Smart pool route your fund to pools with the most competitive return in the market.

Products

DeFi aggregator

The DeFi Aggregator includes:

1. Swap

Place orders in the exchange, trade your tokens, or Myltiply your assets quickly.

2. Liquidity Mining

It's no secret that in the crypto market, liquidity is a scarce resource. We took lessons from this bull market and applied it in our own Swarm.fi DeFi Aggregator when developing our own protocol.

3. Pool

Swap your tokens, add tokens to a pool to earn fees, or list tokens.

4. Save

Earn savings on your crypto by locking it into Save. Automatic and non-custodial.

5. Borrow

Lock your tokens as collateral to generate a decentralized stablecoin soft-pegged to 1 USD.

6. Fund

Use an actively managed and diversified portfolio of the top tokens.

7. Vote

Gathering potential projects to the DeFi pool based on community user opinions and initiating a vote.

8. OTC transactions

Apply another option for adding liquidity to DeFi pools by providing liquidity from Over-The-Counter (OTC) trades.
